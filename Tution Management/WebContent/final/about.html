Rules

1. The Course Fee is to be paid in 2/3 instalments, in consecutive months.
2. In case of loss of identity card an amount of Rs. 10/- will be charged for the issue of duplicate identity card.
3. Fees once paid will not be refunded or transferred under any circumstances, whatsoever.
4. Students should be regular and punctual in attendance. Any absence must be informed in advance to the office Manager / Authorised Teachers
5. except in case if illness where a medical certificate should be produced by the student.
6. Students presence is compulsory for all the Examinations. Any absence for exams will be strictly dealt with.
7. When parents want to discuss any matter they are advised to meet the subject Teacher or the Branch Incharge personally.
8. Parents should avoid making phone calls to resolve any issues.
7. If any student found to have misbehaved in any manner, shall be discharged from the classes immediately.
8. In such events no refund of any part of fees for the remaining period will be made.
9. No students shall enter the classroom with a cellphone.
10. Students can bring cycle or any other vehicle on their own responsibility.
11. Parents must ensure that students are prompt in attending classes / exams and in orderly behaviour.
